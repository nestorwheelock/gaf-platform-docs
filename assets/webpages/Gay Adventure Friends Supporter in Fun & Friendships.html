About this trip

Become a Supporter of Gay Adventure Friends to allow our group to continue to grow and thrive!



Supporter $30/yr, paid in one payment

Benefits- 

*Discounted activities aka save money even if you only occasionally attend events

*Advanced notice of popular events and trips before General members

*Some Supporter events that are only for members that financially support the group.

*Inclusion on Supporter email list



Frequent Flyer Supporter $60/yr, paid in one payment

Benefits- 

*Discounted activities aka save more money if you attend GAF events and/or trips regularly

*Advanced notice of popular events and trips before Supporter and General members

*Some Supporter events that are only for members that financially support the group.

*Special events just for Frequent Flyer and Gold Star Supporter members

*Inclusion on Frequent Flyer Supporter email list



Gold Star Supporter $180/yr, paid in one payment

Benefits- 

*Discounted activities aka save the most money on events

*First notice of popular events and trips before all other (non-Gold Star) members

*Some Supporter events that are only for members that financially support the group.

*Special events just for Frequent Flyer and Gold Star members

*Very special events just for Gold Star members

*Inclusion on Gold Star Supporter email list



Gay Adventure Friends needs financial support to continue. You're not required to join.  However, if you attend paid events regularly, you will save money over the year.  This approach will allow participation in most events by all members, even those that can only very occasionally participate due to work, family, or living outside SoCal. With this approach, you can pay as you go or save money by joining as a Supporter. Some events will be Supporter Only events. But, many events like most hikes and beach days will continue to be free and open to all.



Supporter- 

You want to support our group, get advanced notice of popular events, save money, and attend some events only for Supporters.



Frequent Flyer Supporter- 

You attend events regularly and want to save more money, attend some events that are only for Frequent Flyer and Gold Star Supporters, and want advanced notice of events that will likely sell-out quickly when posted.  Plus, you want to help support our group. 



Gold Star Supporter-

You believe in the value of the fun and new friendships our group creates and you want to see this group to continue to grow and thrive. This level will receive all the benefits of the Frequent Flyer Supporter, plus get First Notice emails for popular events that often sell-out fast once posted and first choice on AirBnB rooms on trips etc. There will also be occasional very special events only for Gold Star Supporters.

Supporter Annual Membership, $29/yr

This package billed once a year at $29 will make you a Gay Adventure Friends "Supporter" for one year. 

You will receive- 

1- "Supporter Discounts" on some events (these discounts will pay for your membership if you plan to attend paid events even just occasionally and save you money if you attend regularly).

3- Advanced notice of events compared to Basic Members but after Frequent Flyer and Gold Star Members. This will give you an advantage to book popular limited size events.

4- Inclusion on the GAF Supporter Member email list.
