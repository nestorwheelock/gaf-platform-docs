Join us for a morning hike along the stunning Sunset Cliffs coastline. This moderate-difficulty hike covers approximately 4 miles round trip with breathtaking ocean views. We'll explore tide pools, watch for dolphins, and finish at a scenic overlook for group photos. Bring water, sunscreen, sturdy shoes, and a camera.

What to Bring

  • Water (at least 1L)
  • Sunscreen
  • Sturdy hiking shoes
  • Camera
  • Snacks
